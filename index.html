How to Play Rummy 420?

Playing Rummy 420 follows the classic rummy rules but with a few added twists. Here’s how you can get started:

1. Deck & Cards
The game is played with 2 standard decks of 52 cards each. The objective is to form valid sets and sequences.

2. Dealing
Each player is dealt 13 cards. The remaining cards form the draw pile, with one card placed as the open pile.

3. Forming Sets & Sequences
Players must create a valid combination of pure sequences and sets. A pure sequence is mandatory to declare a win.

4. Drawing & Discarding
Players pick one card from the draw pile and discard one to maintain 13 cards.

5. Declaring the Game
When a player arranges all cards in valid combinations, they can declare their game to win.

Tips to Win Rummy 420

To increase your chances of winning in Rummy 420, consider the following tips:

2. Prioritize a Pure Sequence

4. Observe Your Opponent
