What is Dragon Tiger?

Dragon Tiger is a straightforward card game often compared to Baccarat. The game is played with a standard deck of 52 cards, and the objective is simple bet on which side will draw a higher card: the Dragon or the Tiger.

  • Two cards are drawn — one for the Dragon and one for the Tiger.
  • Players place their bets on which side will have the higher card, or if the result will be a tie.
  • The Ace is the lowest card, and Kings are the highest.
  • No jokers or additional cards are used.

    • The simplicity of Dragon Tiger makes it easy for beginners, while the fast-paced rounds keep the excitement high for experienced players.
